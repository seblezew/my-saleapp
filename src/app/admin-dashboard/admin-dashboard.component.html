Admin Dashboard

A Simple Sales Management System (SMS) helps businesses manage their sales processes efficiently.

Customer Management

  • Add, update, and manage customer information
  • Track customer interactions and purchase history

Product Management

  • Manage product listings including descriptions and pricing
  • Track inventory levels and reorder alerts

Order Management

  • Create and manage sales orders
  • Track order status from creation to fulfillment

Invoicing

  • Generate and send invoices to customers
  • Track payment status and manage receipts

Reporting & Analytics

  • Generate sales reports and customer insights
  • Analyze sales trends and performance metrics

User Management

  • Manage user accounts and permissions
  • Track user activity and performance
